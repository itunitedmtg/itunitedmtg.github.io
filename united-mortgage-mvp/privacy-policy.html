Privacy Policy

United Mortgage of America • Effective Date: January 1, 2025

United Mortgage of America, LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values the trust you place in us and is committed to protecting your privacy. As we continue to expand and improve our services, we recognize the importance of safeguarding the confidentiality and security of our customers’ personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your nonpublic personal information (“NPI”). It applies to information we obtain in connection with providing financial services and operating our website.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

Protecting your personal information is fundamental to our business. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal and state regulations to guard your nonpublic personal information. All employees are required to adhere to strict confidentiality standards.

2. Information We Collect

We collect nonpublic personal information about you from a variety of sources as necessary to conduct business and provide services.

a. Information You Provide Directly to Us

  • When you complete applications or forms online or in person
  • Through phone calls, email correspondence, or in-person meetings
  • Via our website or affiliated partner platforms

Examples include:

  • Name, address, email, phone number
  • Social Security Number, date of birth
  • Employment and income information
  • Credit history and financial details

b. Information About Your Transactions With Us or Others

  • Loan terms and payment history
  • Account balances and communications
  • Application status and approval conditions

c. Information From Consumer Reporting Agencies

  • Credit scores, credit reports, and payment history
  • Public records and inquiries related to your creditworthiness

d. Information From Third Parties

  • Employers or financial institutions to verify income, employment, or deposits
  • Lenders providing feedback or decisions on applications you submit through us

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process loan applications and provide mortgage-related services
  • Verify your identity and assess creditworthiness
  • Communicate with you about your account or inquiries
  • Fulfill legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations
  • Improve our services, technology, and customer experience

4. Information We Disclose

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose nonpublic personal information to third parties with your consent:

  • Service providers assisting with loan processing, underwriting, compliance, or customer service
  • Credit bureaus, government agencies, or regulators as required by law
  • In connection with a sale, merger, or acquisition of our business

Your information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or as required by law.

5. Confidentiality and Data Security

We restrict access to your information to employees and service providers who need it to perform their duties. Security measures include encryption, secure storage, and controlled access.

6. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or security. Please review their policies independently.

7. Your Consent

By using our website or submitting information to us, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
